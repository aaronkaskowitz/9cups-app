## Description
Track your daily nutrition with zero friction. 9 Cups helps you hit your vegetable targets every day with a beautiful color ring that fills as you eat.

Designed for people following therapeutic diets for MS and autoimmune conditions, 9 Cups tracks 7 daily nutrition categories:

🥬 Leafy Greens (3 cups)
🌈 Colored Produce — 3 colors daily (Red/Purple, Orange/Yellow, Blue/Black)
🧄 Sulfur-Rich Vegetables (3 cups)
🥩 High-Quality Protein
🌊 Seaweed
🫙 Fermented Foods
🫀 Organ Meats (weekly)

FEATURES:
• Rainbow ring with 5 colored segments that fill as you eat through the spectrum
• Tap + on any category card to log food instantly
• 3 protocol levels (Foundation, Paleo, Paleo Plus)
• Complete food reference guide for every category
• Weekly summary with daily scores and streaks
• Meal timing guidance for each level
• Dark mode, high contrast, MS-friendly design
• Large tap targets for accessibility
• VoiceOver support throughout
• 100% local — your data never leaves your device
• No account required, no email, no signup
• Completely free, no ads, no subscriptions

Built by someone who lives with MS and follows this diet every day.

Inspired by the Wahls Protocol® for MS and autoimmune conditions. Not affiliated with Dr. Terry Wahls. This app is not a substitute for medical advice. Consult your healthcare provider before making dietary changes.
